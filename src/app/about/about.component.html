About Us

Creative Shop

Welcome to Creative Shop, your number one source for all things: shoes, bags, t-shirts, jeans and many other. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our products, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness.

Founded in 2005 by Maja Zarkova, Creative Shop has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Maja first started out, her passion for fashion and clothes drove her to quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the fashion, industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Maja Zarkova, CEO, Founder
